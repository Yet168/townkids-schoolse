About Us

ស្ថាបនិកបង្កើតសាលាថោនឃីដស៏កម្ពុជា

Sopheang joined the school as School Manager in 2010 Sopheang has developed strong leadership and motivation skills through his many years' experience in management and administration and teaching with various international organizations, private companies, and in particular, the universities in Cambodia. During that time, he held several key positions including Translator & Admin assistant, Branch Manager of Schools, and Lecturer of the universities. Sopheang has over 20 years’ experience in the educational sector. Sopheang holds a Master degree in International Business (MIB) and two Bachelor degrees in Teaching English as Foreign Language (TEFL) and Computer Science (networking). Sopheang attended various trainings and workshops including business projects, effective leading and managing Teams, effective performance management, leadership, and others.
